# Team Working Agreement
## 1. Working Hours & Online Requirements
- Working days: 10:00–12:00, 14:00–17:00, 19:00–21:00
- Ensure 2–4 hours of effective project time per day
- Leave must be reported 24 hours in advance
## 2. Communication & Response Time
- Tools: WeChat / GitHub
- Respond within 1–2 hours during working hours
- Respond within 12 hours outside working hours
- Respond promptly to urgent issues
## 3. Task & Responsibility Assignment
- Each task shall have a clear owner, deadline and completion criteria
- Owners shall proactively drive and update progress
- Delays must be notified in advance
## 4. Tools & Workflow
- Adopt Git + Pull Request workflow
- All code must be reviewed by at least one other team member before merging
- Direct push to main / dev branches is prohibited
## 5. Meeting Schedule
- Sprint Planning
- Daily Stand-up
- Sprint Review
- Retrospective
## 6. Conflict Resolution
- First communicate internally
- Coordinate by Scrum Master
- Vote or decide by Product Owner if necessary
## 7. Work Discipline
- Complete tasks on time
- Communicate proactively
- Ensure code quality
## 8. Transparency
- All work must be traceable (commit / PR / kanban)
- Private bypass of the process is prohibited
