How to Play

Goal

Score more points than your opponent by forming valid English words on the 15×15 board. This version uses the ENABLE word list for validation.

Setup

Each player draws seven letter tiles from the bag. The first word must cover the center star (H8). Later words must touch existing letters.

Your turn

  • Play: Place one or more tiles from your rack in a single row or column with no gaps, then press Play.
  • Blank (*): Choose any A–Z letter; it scores 0 but counts as that letter.
  • Pass: Skip your turn (contributes to a possible four-pass end).
  • Exchange: Turn on Exchange Mode, select rack tiles, then Exchange Selected (only while at least seven tiles remain in the bag).

Scoring

Letter values are printed on tiles. Premium squares apply only when a tile is first placed there: DL/TL multiply the letter; DW/TW multiply the whole word. Using all seven tiles in one play adds a 50-point bingo bonus.

Winning

  • Classic: When the bag is empty, the first player to use every tile ends the game; remaining tile values are subtracted and (usually) added to the player who went out.
  • Stalemate: If both players pass twice in a row (four consecutive passes), the game ends and rack penalties are applied.
  • Round limit: A round is one move by each player (play, pass, or exchange). After 20 full rounds, the game ends immediately and the higher score wins (ties are draws). Rack penalties are not applied for this ending.

Online play

Start the match server, choose Online match, and join the queue with a second browser or friend. The server keeps the official board, scores, and bag.
